Entities

The term "entity" covers a wide variety of beings, from friendly inhabitants with human-like intelligence, to mindless beasts, to creatures whose motivations and abilities are beyond our understanding.

Each entity has unique behaviors, habitats, and physiological needs. When in doubt, a good rule of thumb for interacting with entities is to leave them be, for your sake and theirs. As always in the Backrooms, trusting your gut is the best way to survive.
