Entity 3 - "Smilers"


A photo of a Smiler before the disappearance of this Wanderer.

Entity Number: 3

Habitat

Majority of Levels

Description

Smilers are generally hostile and can be identified by their reflective eyes and gleaming teeth in the dark. They tend to reside in dark areas on Level 2 and Level 3, and their base is known as "The Smiling Room". The best way to escape a Smiler is to indextain eye contact.

Behaviors

Smilers are attracted to light and will chase any visible light source. They only attack if you panic, retreat, or make loud noises. Slowly moving away while keeping eye contact increases your chance of survival. Using a Smiler Exterminator causes temporary disorientation or retreat.

Biology

Smilers have a long smile with multiple sharp teeth and white glowing eyes. Their full biology is unknown, though rumors suggest non-human, alien-like bodies with unnaturally bent limbs. The M.E.G. is researching further.

Discovery

The Smiler is common across many levels. Modern reports began with an anonymous post describing a "Glowy Smiling Thing". Earlier groups like The Lost referred to them as "The Ones That Smile".

Do's and Don'ts

Do:

  • Keep eye contact and walk slowly.
  • Reindex quiet; avoid loud noises.
  • Use a Smiler Exterminator bottle.
  • Turn off all personal light sources.

Don't:

  • Run away panicking unless chased.
  • Make loud noises.
  • Keep light sources on.
