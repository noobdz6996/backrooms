The first picture ever taken of Level 0.

Level 0 is the 1st level of the Backrooms, and the first level many encounter.

Description:
Level 0 is a non-linear space, resembling the back rooms of a retail outlet. All rooms appear uniform with yellowish wallpaper, damp carpet, and inconsistently placed fluorescent lighting. No two rooms are identical.

Lighting flickers inconsistently and hums at a constant frequency. The buzzing is louder than ordinary fluorescent lights. The carpet substance cannot be safely consumed.

Linear spaces may loop; retracing steps can reveal different rooms. Navigation is extremely difficult. Compasses, GPS, and radios fail.

Level 0 is devoid of life. Contact with other wanderers has never been reported. Deaths are presumed to occur due to dehydration, starvation, and psychological trauma. Rooms with red wallpaper and dim lighting are extremely rare and should not be entered.

Common Hallucinations:
- Humming suddenly stops or increases.
- Doors appear.
- Stairs appear.
- Acute déjà vu.
- Unknown human-like speech.
- Peripheral movement resembling insects.
- Insect-like chittering.
CO2 levels rise steadily; significance unknown.

Entities:
No known entities exist within Level 0. Apparent wanderers are not human.

Entrances and Exits:
Entrances:
Accidental no-clipping from reality is the most common way to enter Level 0. Certain Level 283 doors may also lead here.

Exits:
Exiting is only possible via noclipping, which leads to Level 1. Rarely, entry to the Manila Room is possible; it serves as a dead-end meeting place with M.E.G. documentation.
