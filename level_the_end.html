THE END

Threat Index: Class Beta
- Inconstant Stability
- Inscrutable Security
- Devoid of All Life

The End is a presumably infinite library composed of open areas and wide corridors. Furniture and objects are scattered randomly. Shelves are mostly empty, with occasional meaningless labels or short texts. The building shows temporal decay, with cracks in walls and collapsing shelves. Lights flicker or shut off temporarily. Old computers appear indestructible but rarely function; they briefly display the levelâ€™s name before turning off. Sound is heavily muted. Geometry is distorted, making navigation nearly impossible. Long exposure often results in hallucinations. The End functions as a spatial threshold between levels.

Entrances and Exits

Entrances

Entrances are rare, usually appearing as indestructible wooden doorways without doors. The view beyond is distorted, and sound waves are weaker. Passing the threshold places the wanderer inside The End, and the entrance disappears immediately.

Exits

Exits can occur through noclipping during distortions, transporting the wanderer to the scene presented during that distortion. Another method is to locate a door that leads to a random level.
