Object 2 - "Level Keys"

A stash of Level Keys worth a veritable fortune.

Level Keys are mysterious objects that grant access to certain levels via doors. Each level has its own key, and there can be multiple keys corresponding to the same level. Most resemble 20th-century cast-iron keys, but each carries a unique identifier tied to its home level.

Description

Level Keys can unlock doors within their home level and sometimes allow travel to their home level from other locations. They function similarly to Frontrooms keys but exhibit anomalous properties. The precise mechanics of these keys are still under active investigation by the M.E.G.

Properties

Level Keys resonate at specific frequencies depending on the distance to their home level. This property is being studied to potentially allow level navigation. Keys from the Frontrooms vibrate faintly, hinting at a possible exit. Keys can be broken into shards for compact storage, but shards cannot open doors. Some keys may induce dreams of random levels when held during sleep.

Locations

Level Keys are only found in their respective levels, often hidden or difficult to locate. They are rare and highly valued for bartering. Each level possesses its own Level Keys, though the exact quantity per level reindexs unknown.
