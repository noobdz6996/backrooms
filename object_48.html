Object 48 - "Liquid Pain"

Photo of Object 48

Liquid Pain is an acidic substance with a translucent, red hue. It is extremely dangerous to wanderers due to its fatal effects. Handle only under M.E.G. supervision with proper equipment.

Liquid Pain is a variation of Almond Water. It results from a chemical reaction producing hydrochloric acid and potassium cyanide. This reaction occurs randomly and has been linked to Liminal Echo.

Though similar in smell and density to Almond Water, Liquid Pain has a faint red hue, possibly caused by Liminal Echo’s phenomenon, Autocorrect. Extremely rare, it can appear on any level at any time.

Liquid Pain bears superficial similarities to Cashew Water, but they are largely unrelated.

Description

Properties

The specific effects of ingestion remain uncertain. Collected data suggests death occurs approximately 3 hours post-consumption. Symptoms may include:

  • Heartburn
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Soreness/dryness of the oral cavity
  • Bloody coughing and/or vomiting
  • Severe chest, stomach, and abdominal pain
  • Fatigue

These effects arise from hydrochloric acid damaging tissue and potassium cyanide entering the bloodstream. Death can occur within minutes once poisoning completes. Wounds may also form from the acid.

Do’s and Don’ts

Do:

  • Treat any wounds immediately
  • Report discovery to the M.E.G.
  • Label found bottles of Liquid Pain

Don’t:

  • Make physical contact
  • Drink Liquid Pain or any red-tinted Almond Water
  • Handle without supervision
