Objects

Objects, also known as items or artifacts, are tools, weapons, consumables, and other things used or found by people in the Backrooms.

Many of them follow the rules of normal reality, while others are real-world objects altered fundamentally by the Backrooms' strangeness, and some are created by wanderers for use by themselves or others.

Some objects are dangerous, while others are crucial to survival. Identification of objects is extremely important, as look-alikes exist.

The key difference between an entity and an object is that entities are alive.
